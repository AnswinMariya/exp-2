Google Chrome

Google Chrome is a fast, secure, and widely used web browser developed by Google. It is known for its speed and simplicity.

Mozilla Firefox

Mozilla Firefox is a free and open-source browser that emphasizes privacy and customization. It supports many extensions and developer tools.

Microsoft Edge

Microsoft Edge is based on Chromium and offers seamless integration with Windows, enhanced security features, and speed.
